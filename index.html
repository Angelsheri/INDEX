A layered approach to security.

Security at Stratum is delivered through layered controls that protect your service at every stage, from secure foundations to rapid response.

Foundational Security

Security is built in by default. Hardened configurations, protected networks, controlled access, and resilient backups create a secure baseline before any additional controls are applied.

Preventative Security

Additional protections layered around your service reduce attack surface and strengthen defences as your environment scales, so growth never introduces new vulnerabilities.

Detection & Monitoring

Continuous monitoring identifies threats early. We analyse activity across your service and alert you to potential issues before they impact performance, availability, or data.

Response & Recovery

If an incident occurs, our team acts quickly to contain, investigate, and recover. We support restoration and remediation to improve your posture after every event.
