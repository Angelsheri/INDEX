Our Approach to Multi-Layered Security

Our security at Stratum is delivered through layered controls that protect your service at every stage, from secure foundations to rapid response.

1

Foundational Security

Security is built into our service's by default. We secure your infrastructure with hardened configurations, protected networks, controlled access, and resilient backups, creating a secure baseline before any additional controls are applied.

2

Preventative Security

We help you reduce risk by layering additional protections around your service. These preventative measures reduce attack surface and strengthen defenses as your environment scales, ensuring that growth does not introduce new vulnerabilities.

3

Detection and Monitoring

Our team of AI experts provide continuous monitoring to identify threats early. We analyse activity across your service and alert you to potential issues before they impact performance, availability, or data.
